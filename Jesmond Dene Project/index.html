Welcome to Jesmond Dene Zoo!

Jesmond Dene Zoo can be found next door to the visitor centre in Millfield House, which also houses a café and toilet. It is home to goats, sheep, peacocks, rabbits, and more.

We welcome you to come and visit to look at the animals, but please DO NOT feed them as they are on specially designed diets.

Opening Hours

Jesmond Dene Zoo is open 11am – 3pm everyday, including weekends.

Please note that dates and times are provisional and may be subject to change. Jesmond Dene Zoo may be shut at short notice if the staff are called away to an emergency.

We are closed on Bank Holidays

Our Animals

Jesmond Dene offers a wide range of wildlife and history to offer. Take a walk through the luscious forest and hear the calls of the wild birds or stroll down by the ouseburn river and catch a glimps of the otters. For the first time in living memory, wild otter cubs have been born in the dene. A great example of our efforts to restore biodiversity to Newcastles remaining natural landscapes. If searching for these animals isn't for you, then please join us at our visitor center and see the small zoo of friendly animals we have!

Take a look at a few of the animals we have to offer

We have 14 rabbits in two different enclosures. These are available for holding but please ask a handler to assist as some are very shy!

Come meet Sam and Joe, our two very excitable hogs. But please do not try to ride them.

Our sheep have all been born inside Jesmond Dene and have happily lived within the zoo for three generations now. They're very used to guests and will be happy for a pet or two.

Our peacocks are perhaps the quietest animals in the zoo but if you're lucky and bird feed is out then you might catch the beautiful colours of their feathers.

Our two billy goats have recently had a kid! Please be careful not to get too close as the little tyke is certain to test his horns on you.

Where we are

Come and join the fun!
